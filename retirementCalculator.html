How This Calculator Works

This Bitcoin retirement calculator helps you check whether your current BTC holdings and monthly BTC investment is enough to retire for the future using the following inputs:

  1. Your Current Age & BTC Stack: It starts with your age today and the Bitcoin you currently hold.
  2. BTC Price Growth: The forecasted Bitcoin price is projected using your chosen forecast model (Saylor Bear/Base/Bull, power law, or a custom CAGR).
  3. Monthly BTC Investment: We assume you dollar cost average by buying Bitcoin monthly and therefore increasing your Bitcoin holdings until retirement. We also assume you stop investing in more Bitcoin when you retire.
  4. Retirement Expenses: Based on your ideal monthly expenses at retirement, we sell enough Bitcoin each month to cover those costs (or, depending on your chosen withdrawal strategy, sell in larger chunks). These monthly expenses grow at the inflation rate assumption which you can change in the Assumptions dropdown section.
  5. Withdrawal Strategy: Optimized sells only the BTC you need each month. Conservative sells your whole stack to cash at retirement. Cyclical sells in chunks every few years. You can switch strategies in the Assumptions section.
  6. Retirement Check: Finally, the calculator simulates every month from today to your life expectancy age and reports whether your Bitcoin stack survives the full drawdown — and, if not, roughly what age it runs out.
